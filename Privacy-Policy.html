This Privacy Policy explains how RSI PRO collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app. 1. Contact App name: RSI PRO Email: founderofinnovators@gmail.com 2. Information We Collect Account information, such as your email address and backend account identifiers. Authentication-related information needed to sign in, maintain a session, and reset your password. Alert data you create, such as ticker symbol, interval, time period, thresholds, trigger state, and related metadata. Device and installation data, such as push notification token, installation identifier, and app version. Basic technical and analytics data used to operate, secure, and improve the app. 3. How We Use Information To create and manage user accounts. To authenticate users and maintain account sessions. To store, sync, update, and delete alert configurations. Service Providers RSI PRO uses third-party services that may process data on our behalf, including Back4App / Parse Server for backend storage, authentication, account data, and cloud processing, Firebase for push messaging and analytics, and market or profile data providers used by app features.
